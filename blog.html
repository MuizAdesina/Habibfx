From POS Operator to Forex Mentor – The Habib FX Journey

Published on 5 min read

Introduction

Before becoming a successful forex trader and mentor, I started my journey like many others — from the ground up. I didn't begin as a forex trader. I started with a simple POS (Point of Sale) business, learning how money flows, how people manage transactions, and how to stay disciplined in business.

How I Discovered Forex

It was during my time running the POS business that I first heard about forex trading online. At first, it sounded confusing, but I knew the potential was huge. I took time to learn, study, fail, and grow.

The Turning Point

The real shift came when I stopped chasing fast money and focused on mastering support and resistance strategies, proper risk management, and the trading mindset. From there, things changed.

Why I Teach Today

I started Habib FX to help others avoid the mistakes I made. From one-on-one mentorships to free community lessons, I'm building a movement — a generation of traders who know how to grow capital, stay consistent, and become financially free.

What You'll Learn Here

On this blog, I'll be sharing:

  • Beginner-friendly trading tips
  • Market analysis and trade breakdowns
  • Lessons from my personal trading journey
  • Motivation for traders at every level
  • Student success stories
  • Behind-the-scenes of Habib FX growth

If you're reading this and wondering if you can do it — yes, you can. Just like I did. From the street to the charts. From zero to trader.
